Meet Andy!

Andy is a current student at the University of Michigan pursuing a BFA in Theatre: Design and Production. Through a combination of traditional learning and hands-on production experience, he is passionate about learning all concentrations of theater including lighting, props, scenery, costuming, and sound, with a primary focus in stage management.

With his varied experiences and backgrounds, he can easily adapt to new situations and quickly learn on his feet. He believes knowing a little about everything aids in a smoother production experience for all involved.

Currently, Andy is working as the Sound Script intern at The Muny in St. Louis, Missouri!

As a person who is always excited to learn the next thing, he is open to all new opportunities and is eager to continue growing as a person and theater artist through the rest of his life.
